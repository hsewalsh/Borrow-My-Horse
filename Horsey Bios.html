About Us

A flexible alternative to horse ownership

If you are an experienced rider looking to get involved with horses but cannot make the time or financial commitment that horse ownership requires we are able to offer you a flexible alternative. We have two fantastic horses based near Ongar in West Essex available to experienced riders on a pay as you go basis. Take a look at our horses and contact us for an appointment to meet your future steed!

Mitzi

The School Mistress

Mitzi is a 17.1HH dark bay Dutch Warmblood mare foaled in 2000. Mitzi is a talented mare who was bought by us in 2015 for Renaud to learn to ride on. She had already taught her previous owner how to ride who took her eventing with much success. Mitzi is also a talented showjumper and has even competed in the British National Amateur Finals at Aintree. Mitzi is a no nonsense sort of character, she is excellent in traffic and likes to work hard and play hard!

Oscar

The Big Friendly Giant

Oscar is a 17.3HH bay Irish Sports Horse Gelding foaled in 2000. Oscar has been owned by us since 2011 and is well and truly part of the family. He has turned his hoof to many different jobs over the years including hunting, eventing, showjumping and dressage. Oscar is a firm favourite for hacking as he is excellent in traffic and very well behaved.
